<h1 align="center">🔎 Athoor | عثور</h1>

<p align="center">
A Lost & Found mobile application that helps users report and find lost items easily.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 About The Project</h2>

<p>
Athoor is a smart application designed to help users report lost or found items 
in an easy and organized way. Users can add item details, upload images, 
share locations, and follow the status of their reports to increase the chance 
of finding missing items.
</p>

<h2>✨ Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>User registration and login</li>
  <li>Create lost/found reports</li>
  <li>Add item images and details</li>
  <li>Location-based reports</li>
  <li>Search and view reported items</li>
  <li>Notifications for report updates</li>
  <li>User profile management</li>
</ul>

<h2>🛠 Technologies Used</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Swift / SwiftUI (iOS Development)</li>
  <li>Firebase Authentication</li>
  <li>Firebase Database</li>
  <li>Google Maps API</li>
  <li>Figma (UI/UX Design)</li>
</ul>

<h2>🎯 Project Goal</h2>

<p>
The goal of Athoor is to provide a simple platform that connects people who lost
their belongings with people or organizations that can help them find them.
</p>
